Our Story

{{ info.company_name }} was founded in 2025 by Sudhan Ponnuthurai, a mobile industry expert with over a decade of experience. The company was created to solve a growing problem — kids being consumed by smartphones.

Seeing how endless scrolling and social media were harming focus, Sudhan set out to build something different: a safe, distraction-free phone for students.

The result? The {{ info.product_name }} — no social media, no video platforms, just essential tools and brain-boosting games. Based in Singapore, we're proud to empower families, educators, and schools with tools that support young minds — not distract them.

Because we believe focus is freedom.

Our Mission

To help students achieve their full potential by providing technology that promotes focus rather than distraction. We are committed to creating products that support education and healthy development while giving parents peace of mind.

Our Vision

To create a world where technology enhances learning rather than hindering it. We envision a future where students can harness the benefits of mobile technology without falling prey to its distractions, allowing them to develop healthy digital habits that will serve them throughout their lives.
